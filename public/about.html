Meet AYOOFOSUN

I’m David, a photo retoucher who’s worked on over 10,000 images for studios across continents. It all started as a kid, outshining my brother at Photoshop and realizing I had a gift. After graduating from Federal Government College, Ikot Ekpene, life tested me—losing my dad and narrowly missing my JAMB cutoff. Instead of stalling, I dove into photo retouching, training at CFAT Studios in Aba and grinding as an intern at Vicky Henry Studios.

Editing 10–15 pictures a day sharpened my skills, but it was a viral retouch for Detty Studios that changed the game—thousands of likes and job offers from around the globe. Now, I’m chasing a Film Production degree at Obafemi Awolowo University, blending my passion for visuals with storytelling. My mantra? Keep learning, push boundaries, and stay consistent
